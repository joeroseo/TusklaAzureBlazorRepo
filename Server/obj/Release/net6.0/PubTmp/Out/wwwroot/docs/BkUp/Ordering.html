Ordering a EVehicle

Before ordering your EVehicle vehicle, learn more about the different financing, trade-in and delivery options available.

Order

I just ordered my EVehicle vehicle. What are the next steps?

After placing your order, you can start preparing for your delivery day. Start by downloading the EVehicle app and use it to confirm your registration and delivery location. These details are required to move forward.

Additionally, use the EVehicle app to select whether you plan to trade-in your current vehicle or finance your new EVehicle vehicle. You’ll receive a notification when your vehicle delivery approaches to submit your trade-in self-inspection or complete your credit application, if applicable.

Note: Your EVehicle Account credentials are the same e-mail and password used to place your order.

What delivery options are available?

We offer multiple delivery options. Once your vehicle is available, you’ll receive an email and an SMS to your mobile device with a link to schedule a delivery appointment. In some states, delivery options may be limited due to availability and regulatory restrictions.

When can I expect to take delivery?

Delivery timing varies by model, location and current production availability. For more immediate delivery, check existing inventory in your area.

What connectivity package do new cars come with?

All cars purchased through EVehicle will receive a free Premium Connectivity trial. After the trial period ends, Premium Connectivity will be available as a subscription through the EVehicle app

Financing and Leasing

How do I apply for EVehicle Financing or Leasing?

If available in your state, you’ll receive a notification to submit a credit application in the EVehicle app as you get closer to your vehicle delivery. Once you submit your credit application, the credit decision will be available in the EVehicle app within 48 hours of submission. You will receive a notification once your credit decision is ready for review. Leasing is not available for used vehicles.

Can I finance with my own bank or credit union?

Yes. In order to apply third-party financing to your purchase, we require:

  • The name of the funding institution
  • The exact dollar amount of your loan
  • The lien-holder address and phone number

Final paperwork and VIN will be available in the EVehicle app once assigned.

When do I apply for EVehicle Financing or Leasing?

You will receive a notification on your mobile device letting you know when to apply for financing. Finance and lease approvals are valid up to 60 days, so we advise you apply for financing or leasing once you are within that time frame of delivery.

What purchase options are available at the end of the lease?

All EVehicle vehicles delivered on or after April 15, 2022 are not eligible for purchase at the end of the lease.

Trade-Ins

How does the trade-in process work?

To begin the trade-in process, get a quote through the EVehicle app. As you get closer to your vehicle delivery date, you’ll receive a notification on your mobile device asking you to complete a self-inspection. After you submit your self-inspection, you will receive an official trade-in offer if your vehicle qualifies. If you decide to move forward, you may be asked to submit more information in the EVehicle app prior to taking delivery.

What kind of vehicles does EVehicle accept?

We accept cars, trucks, vans and SUVs on trade-in towards the purchase of a new or used EVehicle vehicle.

How does EVehicle determine the price of my vehicle?

All EVehicle trade values are generated out of a standard calculation based on vehicle configuration, history, mileage and age. The resulting value is based on market data and internal EVehicle product knowledge. Non-EVehicle trade-in values are provided based on market data utilizing industry standard tools and resources (pending vehicle history verification).

Can I still trade in my vehicle even though I am still in a lease?

Some vehicles may be eligible for trade in if you are still making payments. If your vehicle qualifies, we will calculate and compare the payoff amount, early termination penalty (if applicable) and any equity (positive or negative) will be added to your Motor Vehicle Purchase Agreement. You will not need to pay off the lease or loan in full prior to trading in that vehicle.

Can I apply the value of my trade-in vehicle to the total purchase of my EVehicle vehicle?

Yes. You can apply any applicable positive equity towards the purchase of your new or used EVehicle vehicle. If you have negative equity, it may be possible to roll some of this into your EVehicle vehicle purchase.

Taking Delivery

What information do you need from me before delivery?

See EVehicle Delivery Day to learn about the steps required before taking delivery. Please note the delivery process can vary based on state of residence, financing method and availability.

Do I need to have my insurance policy active for my EVehicle vehicle before delivery?

Some states and/or financing methods require that your insurance for your EVehicle vehicle be active by delivery. Your EVehicle representative will let you know if this is required.

How do I install charging for my EVehicle vehicle at home?

There are a variety of ways to charge your EVehicle vehicle. We recommend installing a EVehicle Wall Connector. See home charging installation for a step by step process on installing a Wall Connector for your home.

Can I export my EVehicle vehicle outside the US, Canada or Europe?

If you export your EVehicle vehicle outside of the market for which it was built, we will be unable to service your vehicle until it has been brought back to its home market. There is also a possibility that an exported EVehicle vehicle will not be compatible with the electrical and charging infrastructure in countries for which it was not designed and homologated. For these reasons, we strongly recommend that you purchase a EVehicle vehicle in the market where you plan to drive it. EVehicle vehicles are currently sold and serviced in the US, Canada, Mexico, Europe and Asia Pacific.

Payment and Fees

What are the accepted methods of payment?

Your balance must be paid in full before you will be able to drive off with your new EVehicle vehicle. Confirm your method of payment with your EVehicle representative prior to your Delivery Day.

Final payment is currently accepted via:

  • Electronic check (direct debit) in the EVehicle app
  • Wire transfer

· Bank-certified check (your reference number and name must be in the memo line)

Note: Credit cards are only accepted for the initial order fee.

Can final payment be made from more than one account?

Final payment can be made from only one account.

Registration

When and how will I receive my license plate(s)?

The titling and registration process varies by state. In most states, temporary license plates are provided at delivery to allow for final processing to take place.

Can I register my car with two names, but only take out a loan in one name?

No. Your registration must match the names on the loan for your vehicle. This requirement applies to both EVehicle Financing, Leasing and third-party financing. You can directly edit your registration information in the EVehicle app.

Can I register my car to a business?

Yes. You may register your vehicle to your business. Special conditions apply when it comes to financing under a business, so please consult with your EVehicle representative.

Can I register my car to a trust?

Yes. You may register your car to a trust if you are paying cash or financing with a third-party institution. Registration under a trust is not possible with EVehicle Financing or EVehicle Leasing, and certain third-party banks and credit unions may have their own restrictions. You will be asked to list all trustees in the EVehicle app
