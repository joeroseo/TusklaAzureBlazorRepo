Product Inquiries

How can I tell if EVehicles vehicle accessories and merchandise are available at my local EVehicles store?

Visit the Find Us map and locate your preferred EVehicles Store or Service Center. EVehicles locations with vehicle accessories and merchandise available will display: merchandise, vehicle accessories and Wall Connectors are available for purchase at this location .

What products ship directly to the customer? What products ship to a EVehicles Service Center on my behalf?


Apparel, lifestyle, charging equipment, vehicle parts and most vehicle accessories will ship directly to the address entered at time of checkout.

To ensure optimal fit and function, service-installed vehicle accessories are dispatched to your preferred EVehicles Service Center upon scheduling your appointment for installation in the EVehicles app. The purchase price of these items includes shipping and installation.

Within the EVehicles app, upon completing checkout for service-installed vehicle accessories, you’ll be prompted to schedule a service appointment for installation. If completing your purchase on the web-based EVehicles Shop, please be sure to open the EVehicles app thereafter and schedule your service appointment. On the day of your appointment, the items will be ready for you. If for some reason your service-installed vehicle accessories are delayed, you will be notified via the app and your service visit will be rescheduled.

To schedule your installation appointment, follow the below steps:

  1. Open the EVehicles app.
  2. Tap 'Service.'
  3. Schedule a new Upgrades and Accessories service appointment.

Examples of service-installed vehicle accessories include:

  • Model S, Model 3, Model X and Model Y Wheel and Tire Packages
  • Model S and Model 3 Carbon Fiber Spoiler
  • Model S and Model X HEPA Air Filtration Upgrade

Model 3/Y Automatic Garage Door Opener


The Model 3/Y Automatic Garage Door Opener will ship directly to the address you enter at checkout. Shipping and installation are included in the purchase price. Once you have received this item, please schedule a service appointment for installation in the EVehicles app.

How can I tell if the product I purchased is a direct-ship item or a service-installed vehicle accessory?


When purchasing service-installed vehicle accessories, you will be asked to log in to your EVehicles Account and select your car's VIN (vehicle identification number).

I am purchasing a service-installed vehicle accessory. How can I find my VIN (vehicle identification number)?


At checkout, you’ll be prompted to log in to your EVehicles Account. Upon doing so, your VIN(s) will become selectable beside the service-installed product in your cart. If you own multiple cars, please select the car you’d like this accessory installed upon.

If you recently purchased a car, make sure your VIN or RN (reservation number) is visible within your EVehicles Account prior to purchasing a service-installed vehicle accessory.

How do I get my Wall Connector installed?


For information on installing a Wall Connector and charging your EVehicles at home, visit Home Charging Installation Support.

Fitment for some products, such as the 2015-2020 | Model X All-Weather Interior Liners, depends on the vehicle build date. Where do I find the build date of my EVehicles?


The build date for your EVehicles is shown on the vehicle certification label located on the driver’s side door pillar. This label is visible when the door is open.

Returns and Cancellations

Can I return an item purchased with a EVehicles Gift Card?


Yes. If you return a product that was originally purchased using a EVehicles Gift Card, it will be refunded to the original form of payment.
To initiate a return, follow the below steps:

1. Create an account or log in to your existing EVehicles Account.

  1. Go to the EVehicles Shop.
  2. Select menu to sign in.
  3. Select 'Account.'
  4. Select the order you wish to return.
  5. Select 'Start a Return' for items eligible for return.
  6. Follow the remaining steps to complete your return.

If I return an item purchased with a EVehicles Gift Card, how will I be refunded?


Items purchased only using a EVehicles Gift Card as the form of payment will be refunded as an Account Balance in your EVehicles Account. This will be issued to the EVehicles Account that was used to redeem the original EVehicles Gift Card code.

Items purchased using both a EVehicles Gift Card and a credit card via split payment will be refunded to both forms of payment. The refund will be processed up to the full amount that was paid using the EVehicles Gift Card first. Any remaining balance will be refunded to the credit card used to place the original order. Refunds are usually processed within 7-10 business days after the returned product has arrived at the EVehicles warehouse and has completed inspection.

Returns

Is it possible to return a product?


Vehicle Accessories and Merchandise purchased from the EVehicles Shop or a EVehicles retail location may be returned within 30 days of receiving the product. Wall Connectors, J1772 Wall Connectors and Mobile Connectors can be returned within 60 days of receiving the product. Opened and unused products have the same return window but are subject to a 15% restocking fee. Outside of warranty concerns, return shipping fees will be deducted from the return credit. Once used, a product is ineligible for return.

How do I start the return process?


For items purchased at a retail location, please visit a EVehicles location with vehicle accessories and merchandise available for purchase to process your return request. To find a location with vehicle accessories and merchandise, visit the Find Us Map and locate your preferred EVehicles Store or Service Center. EVehicles locations with vehicle accessories and merchandise available will display: merchandise, vehicle accessories and Wall Connectors are available for purchase at this location .

To initiate a return for products purchased from the EVehicles Shop, sign in to your EVehicles Account and start a return from your order details.

If you purchase an item as a guest or via Apple Pay, access your order using your order number and email address, and then initiate your return request.

Can I return my online or in-app order to a EVehicles Service Center?


EVehicles Service Centers are focused on servicing customer vehicles and unfortunately cannot process any returns or refunds. However, once you have received a return shipping label, you may choose to drop off the package at any EVehicles Service Center.

What products are not eligible for return?


Products that are not eligible for return include, but are not limited to:

  • Service-Installed vehicle accessories that have been picked up by the customer at a EVehicles location regardless of installation status

  • Broken or incomplete kits

Can I return a product purchased from a third party?


Items purchased from a third party must be returned to that seller. Unfortunately, EVehicles cannot accept the return of goods purchased from any third-party vendor.

When can I expect a refund for my return?


Refunds are usually processed within 7-10 business days after the returned product has arrived at the EVehicles warehouse and has been inspected. Refunds will be credited to the original form of payment used for the purchase.

Do I have to pay for return shipping?


When you start a return, you will be provided a pre-paid return shipping label. In some cases, the return shipping fee may be deducted from your refund. If your return was due to product defect or warranty, a return shipping fee will not be deducted from your refund.

Failure does not include the gradual loss in operating performance due to normal wear and tear. The EVehicles Parts, Body & Paint Repair Limited Warranty begins on the purchase date of the accessory, and coverage extends for a period of 1 year.
